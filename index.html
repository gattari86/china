For Parents & Teachers

Teaching Tips:

  • Encourage your child to press the audio buttons to hear pronunciations
  • Practice saying the Mandarin phrases together
  • Discuss the images and ask what they see
  • Use the sticker rewards as positive reinforcement
  • Let them explore at their own pace

Accessibility Features:

  • All interactions are keyboard accessible
  • Audio captions provided for all sounds
  • Motion can be reduced for sensitive users
  • Large touch targets (48px minimum)
  • High contrast colors for visibility

Learning Goals:

  • Cultural awareness and appreciation
  • Basic Mandarin vocabulary exposure
  • Visual and audio association skills
  • Fine motor skills (drag and drop)
  • Memory and matching abilities
